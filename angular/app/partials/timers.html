Reminders App

Create timers and bind web-app related reminders to them. When the timer is up a, notification will appear to remind you to complete your action.

Examples:

  • Log time to Freckle
  • Share your thoughts to Yammer
  • Do your hands and neck exercises
  • Commit your code to GitHub
